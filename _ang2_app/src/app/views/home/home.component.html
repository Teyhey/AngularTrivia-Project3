Welcome to Crazy Cash Taxi (Silber Edition)! In this game, test your knowledge, trying to earn as much money as possible in the time limit.

Need to practice?

  • View question counts for each knowledge category on "Categories" page
  • Test your knowledge on the "Questions" page
  • Change difficuly and categories at a click of a button
  • See how your score compares to the questions answered

Ready to play?

  • Visit the "Login" page to register or sign in
  • Go to the "Game" page and select your game mode
  • Take a high-speed tour with Silber by yourself or with others
  • Get 3 questions wrong in a row, and you get kicked to the curb!
  • Go double-or-nothing on a final crowd-sourced question!

Rewards

  • Earn the right to submit a question by winning a game!
  • Compete with your friends to be Silber's best student! ("Scoreboard" page)
  • Redeem CCT cash at your local DoesntExistMart.
